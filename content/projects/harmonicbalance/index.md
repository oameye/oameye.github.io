[HarmonicBalance.jl](https://github.com/QuantumEngineeredSystems/HarmonicBalance.jl)
is a Julia package implementing multi-frequency Floquet expansions to find and
analyse steady states of driven nonlinear (quantum) systems. I am a maintainer
of the package since March 2024.

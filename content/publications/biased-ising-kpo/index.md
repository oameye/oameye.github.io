abstract: We explore the control of coupled Kerr parametric oscillator (KPO) networks
  under external force, demonstrating how the force biases the system's configuration,
  akin to a magnetic field biasing a spin ensemble. The findings provide a method
  to create Ising machines with arbitrary bias, extending to cases unachievable in
  real spin systems.

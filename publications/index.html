Publications

My research focuses on driven-dissipative few-body physics. I apply Floquet perturbative expansions to interacting bosonic systems to extract non-equilibrium stationary states (NESS), then analyse the fluctuations of these NESS via linear-response theory. I also study optimal transition paths between NESS in driven-dissipative systems under Markovian noise.

Please reach out to collaborate.
